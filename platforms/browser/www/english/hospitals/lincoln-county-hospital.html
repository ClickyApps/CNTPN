Hospital Services

Access to a Midwife
A midwife service is available for postnatal ladies from the ward and those who have been discharged home.
If you need to access this service, please speak to the nurse in charge on the unit.

Catering and Shops available on site

  • Restaurant on 1st floor.
  • Various food outlets throughout the hospital.
  • WH Smith and Hairdressers at hospital main entrance.

Cashpoint
Inside WH Smiths near hospital main entrance.
Please note only available during shop opening hours.

PALS
Telephone 01522 707071 open Mon-Fri 8-4, Main Reception Email pals@ulh.nhs.uk.

Interpreters
Interpreters are available through LanguageLine and can be arranged by the unit staff.

Religious & Spiritual Support
There is a chapel within the Hospital. Oncall chaplaincy support is available 24 hours a day providing for all faiths.

Laundry
There are no laundry facilities available on site.

What to expect on arrival

Access to the unit
Entry is via a locked door, ring bell to gain access.

Who to ask for on arrival
Nurse in charge

Who will give initial update on baby's condition
Nurse caring for your baby can give updates at any time.
Medical staff are available during the ward rounds and upon request.

Ward Round times
Every morning starting at 10.30am

Quiet time
Daily between 12.30pm and 2.00pm.

Meal Provision
Food and drink provided to all parents resident on the unit and to breastfeeding mothers.

Self Catering Facilities
Microwave, fridge freezer, crockery and cutlery available.
Cereals, bread and milk are also provided free of charge.

Mobile Phone
It is important that mobile phones are switched to silent and that telephone calls are made away from the cot side, in the public areas of the hospital as this helps to reduce noise levels which babies can find stressful. Mobile phones can be used to take photographs of your baby.

Breast Feeding Facilities/Equipment
Expressing room on the unit.
Mobile breast pumps available for use at the cot side.

Sitting Area
There is a sitting room and a quiet room on the unit.

Play Area/facilities
There are toys available in the parent sitting room. Children should be supervised at all times.
