Visiting Policies

Visiting Hours
Open visiting for parents.
Parents are invited to be included on the discussion and care of their baby on the daily ward round.
Other visitors: 2-3pm and 6pm to 8pm.

Numbers allowed
4 per cot

Siblings
Siblings allowed to accompany parents.

Restrictions
No other children are allowed.

Hygiene procedures for visitors
Hands are cleaned as they enter the neonatal unit, alcohol gel provided at the entrance. Hats and coats must be removed and placed on coat hooks at the door entrance. Hands cleaned again as the parents enter the room.
