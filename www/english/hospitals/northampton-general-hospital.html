Hospital Services

Access to a Midwife
A midwife service is available for postnatal ladies from the ward and those who have been discharged home.
If you need to access this service, please speak to the nurse in charge on the unit.

Catering and Shops available on site

  • Restaurant on ground floor in the main corridor
  • Various WRVS Shops
  • A coffee shop

Cashpoint
On hospital street near the WRVS shop.

PALS

Interpreters
Interpreters and signers are available and can be arranged by the unit staff.

Religious & Spiritual Support
There is a chapel within the Hospital.

Laundry

What to expect on arrival

Access to the unit
[AWAITING INFO]

Who to ask for on arrival
The ward co-ordinator or the nurse caring for your baby.

Who will give initial update on baby's condition
The nurse caring for your baby can give updates at any time.
Medical staff are available during the ward rounds and upon request.

Ward Round times
Monday starting at 9.30am, Tuesday to Sunday starting at 08.45am.

Quiet time
There is no dedicated quiet time. The unit promotes a quiet and peaceful environment at all times

Accommodation for parents
1 double on-suite bedroom is available just off the unit.

Accommodation for siblings
Not available.

Meal Provision
Hot meals provided for parents staying overnight. Breastfeeding mothers can have sandwiches at lunch and supper time.

Self Catering Facilities
Microwave, fridge, crockery and cutlery available. Snack bar containing a variety of biscuits, chocolate, crisps, fruit bars and soup are also provided free of charge

Mobile Phone
It is important that mobile phones are switched to silent and that telephone calls are made away from the cot side, in the public areas of the hospital as this helps to reduce noise levels which babies can find stressful. Mobile phones can be used to take photographs of your baby.

Breast Feeding Facilities/Equipment
There is a Breastfeeding/expressing room on the unit.
Mobile breast pumps are available for use at the cot side.

Sitting Area
There is a sitting room and a quiet room on the unit

Play Area/facilities
There are a small number of toys available in the sitting room.
Children should be supervised at all times
