Visiting Policies

Visiting Hours
Parents are welcome to visit anytime.
Grandparents, significant others and siblings are also welcome anytime but not during quiet time which is 12:30-14:00.
Children must leave by 7pm.
Other visitors 14:00 – 19:30

Numbers allowed
Parents/carers plus 2 others.

Siblings
Anytime apart from 12:30-14:00 and after 19:00 hours.

Restrictions
No children other than siblings.

Hygiene procedures for visitors
Strict hand washing procedures (information provided). Alcohol hand rub is available on entry to the ward. Coats to be hung on pegs outside the clinical area.

Neonatal unit facilities

Waiting area
No specified waiting area. Parents’ sitting room available

Accommodation for parents
3 double bedrooms.

Parent facilities on unit
Sitting room,3 bedrooms, kitchen, bathrooms, toilets and showers.

Accommodation for siblings
None.

Accommodation costs
None.

Nearest B&Bs/hotels for other family members
Lincoln Hotel, Bailgate White Hart Hotel, Bailgate B&B’s in St Anne’s Road.

Access to Unit
Open access for parents. Ring bell on left hand side of main door to speak to the receptionist or unit staff.

Catering/refreshments
Tea, coffee, milk, bread and cereal provided. All meals provided for mothers up to 7 days post delivery. Subsequently subsidised meal vouchers are available for parents.

Cooking facilities
Microwave, kettle, toaster, fridge.

Mobile phone use
It is important that mobile phones are switched to silent and that telephone calls are made away from the cot side, in the public areas of the hospital as this helps to reduce noise levels which babies can find stressful. Mobile phones can be used to take photographs of your baby.

Telephone access
No

Breastfeeding facilities/equipment
Breast expressing room, milk kitchen with fridge and freezer storage

Play area/facilities for siblings
Use of a room with toys but supervision required by parents/carers.

What to expect when you arrive

Who to ask for on arrival

Who will give initial update on baby’s condition and timing?

10.30am
Parents included.

Frequency of updates on baby’s condition and by whom
Ward round - Doctors, ANNPs and/or named nurse.

Quiet time – when & what happens
12.30 – 2.00pm. No medical procedures performed unless urgent, blinds are down, lights are out. This is ‘parent and baby only time’.
