Visiting Policies

Visiting Hours
Parents can visit at anytime.
All other visitors from 1400-1500 & 1800-2000

Numbers allowed
4, including a parent.

Siblings
Yes

Restrictions
Parents welcome to stay during ward round.

Hygiene procedures for visitors
Hands are cleaned as they enter the neonatal unit, alcohol gel provided at the entrance. Hats and coats must be removed and placed on coat hooks at the door entrance. Hands cleaned again as the parents enter the room.

Neonatal unit facilities

Waiting area
At reception for all visitors, parent lounge with TV within unit.

Accommodation for parents
Walnut street nurses accommodation, rooms & flats available at a cost.

Parent facilities on unit
5 bedrooms all en-suite plus 3 have TV’s.

Accommodation for siblings
None.

Costs associated with accommodation
Nil

Location
On LRI site.

Access to Unit
Entry buzzer

Catering/refreshments
Meals can be ordered for mothers who are resident with babies.

Cooking facilities
Microwave available in parents lounge.

Mobile phone use
It is important that mobile phones are switched to silent and that telephone calls are made away from the cot side, in the public areas of the hospital as this helps to reduce noise levels which babies can find stressful. Mobile phones can be used to take photographs of your baby.

Breastfeeding facilities/equipment
Mothers room for expressing milk, also freezers for storage of breast facilities/equipment milk in feeding room.

Play area/facilities for siblings
Yes, play specialist available for sibling support timetable in the parents room.

What to expect when you arrive

Who to ask for on arrival

Who will give initial update on baby’s condition and timing?

Ward round times and whether parents are included
0930-1130 most days except Friday when it starts at 1000-1200. Parents can attend.

Seen by Consultant or Senior Registrar within 24 hours. Updates when required no set frequency.
